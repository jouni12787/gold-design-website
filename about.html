Heritage & Craftsmanship

Maison Chakaroun · Established Beirut 1964

Heritage

Damascus filigree lineage. Our bracelets reinterpret filigree techniques preserved by Hassan Chakaroun, marrying slender wire twists with modern proportions.

Family crest pendants. Pendants echo the cedar crest carried by generations, ready for engraving initials, verses or bespoke monograms.

Curated palettes. Each theme references a chapter of our maison — Desert Dawn, Midnight Majlis and Plum Soirée — with a custom option for your house codes.

Craftsmanship

Maison-approved weights. Real densities for gold, platinum and sterling silver drive the gram estimate so production teams can quote confidently.

Showroom lighting presets. Orbit your 3D jewel through Gallery, Spotlight or Twilight scenes, or bookmark your own camera for export-ready frames.

Saved ateliers. Store favourite builds and palettes locally, then add them to a wishlist for private client previews.
